About Us

AiGentic Group is a holding company that develops AI-powered financial technology and media brands. We operate through a portfolio of synergistic companies, creating innovative solutions that transform industries.

Our unique approach combines deep industry expertise with cutting-edge AI capabilities to identify opportunities, develop solutions, and scale businesses in the financial technology and media sectors.

With headquarters in Australia and a global outlook, we're positioned to capitalize on the rapidly evolving AI landscape while maintaining a focus on sustainable growth and value creation.

Our Approach

AI-First Strategy
